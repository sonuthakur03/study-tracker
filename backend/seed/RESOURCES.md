### Key Topics to Focus (TU Exam pattern)
1. **Fayol's 14 Principles** — write all 14 with examples (long question)
2. **Taylor's Scientific Management** — always in exam
3. **Maslow's Hierarchy** — draw pyramid diagram
4. **Herzberg's Two-Factor Theory** — compare with Maslow
5. **MBO** — Steps of Management by Objectives

### Nepali Context Examples to use in answers
- Nepal Telecom (NTC) — state-owned management example
- Ncell — private company management
- Everest Bank — banking management

| Subject | Guaranteed Long Questions |

| DotNet | C# OOP programs, LINQ queries, difference questions |

| Management | Fayol's principles, Motivation theories, Decision making |
| Computer Graphics | DDA/Bresenham algorithm, 2D transformation matrix |

### Study Group Tips
- Form a group of 4-5 students
- Assign one unit per person → they teach others (best for retention)
- Practice writing answers in exam format — 2 marks needs 2-3 lines, 10 marks needs full page
